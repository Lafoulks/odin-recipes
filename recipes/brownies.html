Brownies

These brownies are soft, easy to make, and delicious! Perfect for a cozy night in with loved ones.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a large mixing bowl, melt 1/2 cup of butter. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanila extract.
  3. Beat in unsweetened cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into a baking pan lined with butter.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutues. Check readiness by inserting a fork in the middle of the brownies. If the fork comes up clean, serve with a side of vanilla ice cream and enjoy!
