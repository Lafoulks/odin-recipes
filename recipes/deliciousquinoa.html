Damn Delicious Quinoa

This dish is as tasty as it is healthy! All the ingredients are cooked in the same pan, making preperation a breeze. Each bite is packed with flurries of flavor, ranging from hints of lime, a kick of pepper, and the refreshing taste of avocado.

Created on this popular website.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno, minced
  • 1 cup of quinoa
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth (may be substituted with chicken broth)
  • 1 (15-ounce) can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (14.5-0z) can of fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of corn, roasted, frozen, or canned
  • 1 teaspon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 avocados, halved, seeded, peeled, and diced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons of cilantro leaves

Directions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium high heat. Add the garlic and jalapeno and cook, stirring frequently for 1 minute.
  2. Mix in the quinoa, broth, tomatoes, beans, corn, cumin, and chili powder. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil and then cover, reducing heat. Let it simmer until quinoa is cooked through, for about 20 minutes. Stir in the avocados, lime juice, and cilantro
  3. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
