Happy Plums

Have you had a stressful day filled with cannon-fire from a deranged man's research project? Too depressed to move? Look no further; this recipe will invigorate you with a happiness so potent your entire outlook on life will be forever changed!

Ingredients:

  • Plums
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter
  • 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick, broken in half
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • Sour cream

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Halve plums and remove the pit.
  3. Place plums in a large dish, sprinkle with brown sugar, and add a spoonful of butter to the inside of each plum. Place plums in oven.
  4. Slowly heat the balsamic vinegar, honey, maple syrup, star anise, and cinnamon for about 15 minutes, ensuring it does not come to a boil. Take the plums out of the oven and drizzle them with the balsamic vinegar mix.
  5. Place plums back in the oven for another 5-10 minutes, until tender.
  6. Remove the plums from the oven. Spoon portions into a mug and top with sour cream. Enjoy the taste of true courage!
