Lemon Waffle

Description

A recipe from one of my favorite video games, Final Fantasy 14.

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk
  • Lemon Juice
  • Egg, beaten
  • Butter, melted and cooled
  • Vanilla Extract
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Kosher Salt
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Zest of Two Lemons
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Fruit, Honey, Powdered Sugar for serving
